Indian Culture

  • Diverse traditions and customs across different regions
  • Rich history dating back thousands of years
  • Colorful festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Navratri
  • Variety of classical dance forms including Bharatanatyam and Kathak
  • Traditional clothing such as sarees and kurta pajamas
  • Flavorful and diverse cuisine with regional specialties
  • Ancient practices like yoga and Ayurveda
  • Strong emphasis on family values and respect for elders
